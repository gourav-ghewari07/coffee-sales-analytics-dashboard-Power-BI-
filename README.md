☕ Coffee Sales Analytics Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

The Coffee Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze coffee shop sales performance and uncover actionable business insights.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visualizations that help understand customer purchasing behavior, product performance, revenue trends, and payment preferences.

---

🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Identify top-selling coffee products.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Discover sales patterns across different times and months.
- Support data-driven business decisions.

---

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI Desktop

---

📈 Key Insights

1. Latte Generated the Highest Revenue

Latte emerged as the most popular product and contributed significantly to total revenue.

2. Card Payments Dominated Transactions

The majority of customers preferred card payments over cash transactions.

3. Peak Sales Occurred During Morning and Afternoon

Customer purchasing activity remained highest during morning and afternoon hours.

4. Monthly Revenue Showed Seasonal Variations

Several months outperformed others, indicating fluctuations in customer demand.

5. Few Products Generated Most Revenue

A limited number of products contributed a large percentage of overall sales.

💡 Business Recommendations

Inventory Management

Maintain sufficient inventory levels for top-performing products.

Product Promotion

Promote lower-performing products through discounts and combo offers.

Payment Experience

Continue investing in digital payment infrastructure.

Workforce Planning

Schedule additional staff during peak sales periods.

Marketing Strategy

Use seasonal sales trends to plan future promotional campaigns.
